Internship

Volvo Cars

During my internship at Volvo, I worked on developing a virtual reality experience for the Volvo 360C, an autonomous vehicle concept. The project involved using a fully equipped VR rig with headsets to simulate the experience of riding in the 360C, focusing on how passengers would feel in a self-driving car environment. Our goal was to make the experience as realistic as possible, capturing the sense of movement and comfort that the 360C aims to provide, while also exploring user interactions within an autonomous ride.
